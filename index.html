The Philosophy
Most programs are built around machines, cable stacks, and comfort. Old Iron is built around iron. The squat rack. The bench. The deadlift platform. The overhead press bar. These four movements have built more muscle and more strength than any piece of equipment invented in the last fifty years. They still will. The machine fits around you. The barbell teaches you to fit around it. That’s the difference.
