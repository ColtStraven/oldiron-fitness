The Philosophy
Most apparel is built around the gym selfie. Old Iron is built around iron. The squat rack. The bench. The deadlift platform. The overhead press bar. These four movements have built more strength and more character than any fitness trend invented in the last fifty years. The gear you wear in the gym should reflect that. No logos for clout. No cuts for the camera. Just durable, functional apparel made for the iron and the people who respect it.
